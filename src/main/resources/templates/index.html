Gain control through the Task-Hub
Task Manager is designed to allow you to streamline your workflow and stay organized. There are a variety of features; including a kanban board like-experience, which allows you to stay organized from an "In Progress" task to a "Complete" task.
The goal is to help you manage your "tasks, projects, goals, etc..." ... with ease, organization, and structure. There are a variety of features to help you manage each task.
The user-friendly interface and task-oriented management features enable you to complete your To-Do's into To-Done's quicker than ever.
