Task Manager is designed to allow you to streamline your workflow and stay organized. There are a variety of features; including a kanban board like-experience, which allows you to stay organized from an "In Progress" task to a "Complete" task. The goal is to help you manage your "...tasks...projects...goals...etc..." with ease, organization, and structure. There are a variety of features to help you manage each task.
The user-friendly interface and task-oriented management features enable you to complete your To-Do's to To-Done's quicker than ever. Whether you're a business owner, freelancer, or just looking to stay organized, Task Manager will boost your efficiency and reduce the feeling of being overwhelmed.
